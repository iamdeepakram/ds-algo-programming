A College wants to develop a software to manage the data of students and professors.
They have the following requirements. Help the college to design classes for their software.

Student:
- Name
- Date of birth
- Address
- Phone number
- Department
- Programme
- Year of joining
- Total fees
- Fees paid
- A student should be able to update their address 
- A student should be able to update their phone number 
- A student should be able to pay fees

Professor:
- Name
- Date of birth
- Address
- Phone number
- Department
- Year of joining
- Qualification
- Salary
- A professor should be able to update their address
