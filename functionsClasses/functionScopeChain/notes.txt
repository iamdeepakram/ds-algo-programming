Question:
Given aperson's age and gender, determine if they are eligible for travel concession. 
A person is allowed concession if they are more than 60 years old.
But, women are allowed concession if they are more than 58 years old.

Input:
First line contains a positive integer, say n, denoting the number of test cases. 
Next n lines contain 2 space separated values each.
- First value is an integer, denoting the age of the person.
- Second value is one of M, F, T, denoting Male, Female and Transgender respectively. 

Output:
n lines containing one string ("Eligible" or "Not Eligible").

Example:
Input
3 
59 F
29 M
37 T 

Expected output:
Eligible 
Not eligible
Not eligible
